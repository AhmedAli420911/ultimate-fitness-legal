Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 3, 2026

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Ultimate Fitness App (hereinafter referred to as "Application" or "App") for mobile devices, developed by Ahmed Ali (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider" or "we"). This service is provided as a Freemium service (free with optional paid subscriptions).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use the Application, we may collect the following information that you provide:

  • Account Information: Name, email address (via Apple Sign In)
  • Fitness Data: Workout sessions, exercise logs, sets, reps, weights
  • Nutrition Data: Food entries, meals, calories, macronutrients
  • Body Measurements: Weight, height, body fat percentage, progress photos
  • Health Goals: Fitness goals, dietary preferences, workout preferences

1.2 Health Data from Apple HealthKit

With your explicit permission, we integrate with Apple HealthKit to access:

  • Step count
  • Active calories burned
  • Sleep data

Important: We only read data from HealthKit. We do not write or share your HealthKit data with third parties. Your HealthKit data is stored securely on your device and is only used to display insights within the App.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

The Application may automatically collect:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifier
  • App usage patterns and analytics
  • Crash logs and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Services: Track your workouts, nutrition, and fitness progress
  • Personalization: Provide personalized workout and nutrition recommendations
  • Sync Across Devices: Store your data securely in the cloud (Firebase) so you can access it from multiple devices
  • Send Notifications: Workout reminders, milestone achievements, streak warnings (only if you enable notifications)
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to improve features and fix bugs
  • AI Features: Use AI to analyze food photos for calorie estimation and provide meal suggestions

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Local Storage: Data is stored on your device for offline access
  • Cloud Storage: Data is synced to Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with end-to-end encryption
  • HealthKit Data: Remains on your device and is never uploaded to our servers

3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Data at rest is encrypted in Firebase
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We use Apple Sign In for secure authentication

4. Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Cloud storage, authentication, crash reporting
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

4.2 Apple Services

  • Sign in with Apple: For secure authentication
  • Apple HealthKit: For health data integration
  • Privacy Policy: Apple Privacy Policy

We do NOT sell your personal data.

We only share your data in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services (Firebase) that help us operate the App

  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of users or the public
